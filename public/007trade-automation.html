007Trade · Automation

The server polls Alpaca on an interval when the master switch is on. Orders use the same path as manual Submit (bracket + auto-qty from your risk profile in Settings). Supported automation: opening-range (long / short / both, optional exit outside the range, flatten before close), momentum family (prior completed day high + optional min breakout %), mean-reversion (buy when price has dropped from the session open by your fade %, after a minimum number of minutes).

Paper first. Live Alpaca accounts require checking "Allow automation on live" — otherwise the runner skips every tick. You are responsible for risk, fills, and regime fit.
