Risk & position sizing

Saved to disk. When Bracket orders is on, each Submit sends a market entry plus Alpaca stop and take-profit prices derived from a live quote and your % below. Auto size sets share count from account equity and “max account risk %” (roughly: how much of the account you are willing to lose if the stop hits). This is not a 24/7 bot — you still press Submit; child orders are managed on Alpaca after the entry fills.
